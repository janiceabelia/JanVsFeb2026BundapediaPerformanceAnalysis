● 11   KEY TAKEAWAYS
@bundapedia.tiktokshop

Three things to carry into May.

01 — REACH

The algorithm finally caught a piece of content.

April's 1.48M views came from a short streak (Apr 21–28). Identify the post format that triggered it and double down — viral lift is repeatable when the trigger is understood.

02 — ENGAGEMENT

Shares outperformed every other action.

Likes grew 10×, shares grew 433×. Audiences are forwarding the content, not just consuming it — a strong organic-distribution signal worth amplifying with paid boost.

03 — AUDIENCE

Decline is reversing; convert curiosity to follows.

Profile-visit rate dropped under viral load, but absolute visits 2.3×'d. Use the bio + pinned content to capture follows from the new traffic — the +303 day on Apr 28 shows it works.
